About Us

Welcome to "Tenho Fome," a non-profit organization that aims to help people worldwide who suffer from hunger and lack of food. We believe that hunger is one of the world's biggest humanitarian crises today, and we are working tirelessly to make donating money and food easier and more transparent.

"Tenho Fome" was founded by a group of people concerned about the growing hunger crisis in the world. We joined forces to create a platform that connects donors and non-profit organizations that work to provide food to those in need. Our goal is to make donating money and food a simple and efficient process for everyone.

We believe in transparency and accountability, and that is why we work hard to ensure that all donations go to the people who really need them. We strive to ensure that all partner organizations we work with have high ethical standards and that all donations are traceable.

At "Tenho Fome," we value diversity and inclusion and aim to create an environment in which everyone can feel welcome and respected. We believe that together we can make a difference and help fight the hunger crisis in the world.

To donate to "Tenho Fome," simply visit our website and select the donation option that best suits your needs. You can donate money, food, or even volunteer to help the organization. All donations are very welcome and will make a big difference in the lives of the people we help.

We thank all of our donors and partners for making our work possible and for helping us achieve our goal of a world without hunger. Together, we can make a difference!
